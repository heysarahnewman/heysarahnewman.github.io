About

I'm a part-time doctoral student at the Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health working toward my Doctor of Public Health, with a concentration in implementation science. My research explores complex personnel health issues in large organizations — how they're understood, how policies get implemented (or don't), and what happens in the gap between intention and practice.

I'm especially drawn to data, qualitative methods, and the role AI can play in public health research. When I'm not studying, I work full-time in the public sector. This site is a place to share my academic work and whatever I'm thinking about right now.
